# ATM Transactions Insights Dashboard

<p align="justify">
This Power BI dashboard offers a structured view of ATM operations and financials using a sample dataset. It can help users monitor costs, revenue, uptime, and monthly transactions while comparing state-level performance and cost allocations.
</p>

## Key Features

- **Data Preparation and Modeling**
  - File ingestion, cleaning and transformation with <b>Power Query</b>, including merges, type standardization, and derived columns.
  - **DAX measure** for calculating average profit percentage.
- **Visuals and Dashboard Design**
  - **KPI cards** for Total Cost (**₹296M**), Avg. Monthly Revenue (**₹52.13K**), Avg. Uptime (**91.89%**), Avg. Monthly Transactions (**60M**), and Avg. Gross Profit (**59.60%**).
  - **State-Revenue Trends** highlighting performance for **Assam, Punjab, Jammu & Kashmir, Manipur, and Tripura**.
  - **Monthly Transactions Analysis** with financial vs non-financial split across **March, August, November, and December 2024**.
  - **Monthly Revenue & Transactions** trend for **Apr, Jul, and Oct 2024**.
  - **Cost allocation donuts** showing categories such as **ATM AMC, UPS AMC, VSAT AMC, CRA**, and **Site Maintenance**.
  - **Button slicer** for month-wise ATM transaction analysis.
  - **Consistent color schemes & effective clutter management** for a visually compelling dashboard.

## Dashboard Insights

- **Operational Snapshot:** Total cost is **₹296M** and average uptime is **91.89%**, which indicates stable operations with good service availability.
- **Revenue and Profitability:** Average monthly revenue is **₹52.13K** and average gross profit is **59.60%**, which shows a positive margin profile across monitored periods.
- **Transaction Activity:** Average monthly transactions are **60M**, and months like **March and August 2024** show higher bars, which indicates steady usage with periodic peaks.
- **State Comparison:** **Assam** and **Punjab** show higher revenue bars compared to other states, which suggests stronger activity in these regions.
- **Cost Distribution:** Cost donuts highlight heavier allocations to **ATM AMC** and **UPS AMC**, while **VSAT AMC** and **Site Maintenance** form smaller shares, which aids prioritization for cost control.
